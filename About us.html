What is Droplet (DPT)?

Droplet is an ERC-20 token running on the Ethereum network. It is used to reward contributors in our forum and early investors and promoters. The future of Droplet is the following:

1. Implement untraceable transactions with hidden addresses.
 
2. Implement and enforce adress tags on all public wallet addresses under creation, for limiting miss type risks when sending tokens, hidden addresses will be unaffected.
 
3. Creation of a decentralized exchange for Droplet and all associated Cryptocurrencies.
 
4. Moving out of the ERC-20 stage and into its own blockchain with its own miners, stakers and its own platform.
