Droplet (DPT) PRE-SALE

Droplet introduction

Droplet is an ERC-20 token, Droplet (DPT) stands for Decentralization, Privacy and Trust. Droplet will have its own decentralized Trust shopping platform, it will integrate Znark technology for privacy and it will have its own Decentralized exchange.

Privacy

Privacy is important in the cryptocurrency space as every transaction is traceable, we will implement Znark technology as soon as we move over to our own blockchain with our own miners and stakers. This will not happen in the near future, but we are already working on a transition from the ERC-20 standard to become an individual Cryptocurrency.

Decentralization

Droplet will have its own decentralized exchange and its own decentralized shopping platform. There is a huge request of decentralized exchanges as the current exchanges are making choices about hardforks of Bitcoin and Ethereum airdrops that many people in the cryptocurrency community doesn't agree upon. The decentralized exchange will be added and open for trading after the end of Droplets public-crowdsale that starts the 1st of January. Decentralization was the idea behind the creation of cryptocurrencies when it was first made, in 2009. We are taking decentralization to the next level with our decentralized platform "Trust" and our decentralized exchange.

Trust Platform

Droplet will have its own platform called "Trust", on "Trust" you can buy and sell what you want with Droplet. We like to call the "trust" platform "Ebay 2.0". The platform will be decentralized and private, no password or username is required. The platform will supply every new user with a set amount of reputation points, called TRUST points. TRUST points will define your account and other users can see your TRUST balance. The reputation points will make it easier to separate theft and fraud from loyal and trustworthy users, TRUST is also required to create or interact with an advertisement. To gain "TRUST", you have to successfully finish an advertisement, you can gain 1-10 TRUST per advertisement, depending on the price. The more TRUST points you have, the higher up on the list of advertisements you get, its done this way to reward loyal and trustworthy users. When a user has 1000 TRUST or more, the TRUST points can be exchanged for Droplet tokens, with a 1000 for 1 ratio. A user will be punished with a negative TRUST balance if the account is suspected for fraud, this makes the user unable to create or interact with advertisements. 20% of all Droplet tokens will be locked up until we have the platform up and going, these Droplet tokens will then only be used for exchanging TRUST reputation points. The Trust platform is needed as there is thousands of cryptocurrencies, but very few places to use them. The trust platform enables actual shopping with cryptocurrencies. The TRUST points is our way to reward contributors of the cryptocurrency network, and motivate them and newcomers to use cryptocurrencies on a daily basis. You can read more about the "Trust Platform" and "TRUST" reputation points in our whitepaper.

Pre-sale information

The PRE-SALE of Droplet is to fund our start budget so that more developers can be hired and so that we can start advertising. There will be a total supply of 1 000 000 tokens, so it will be very limited compared to other coins. 10% of the tokens will be sold in the PRE-SALE. There will be a Crowdsale the 1st of January where 40% will be sold, the price per token will be 0.04 Ethereum, the price for this Pre-Sale is 0.02 Ethereum. A total of 50% of Droplet tokens will be sold in our sales. The other 50% is dealt into 2 pieces: 30% for the campaign "development, advertising, travelling and conferences". The last 20% will be locked up until our Trust platform is built, you can read more about our upcoming Trust Platform in the Whitepaper. This is your opportunity to buy Droplet tokens before they gain their potential value.
